# Overview of delegator 
`Delegator` is a new role introduced in phase 1 of the Zilliqa seed node staking program. With the `Delegator` role, there is a better seperation of roles and privileges. 

Below we summarized the changes to `SSN operator` and the role of `Delegator`.

__`SSN operator` in phase 0__
- Operates the SSN
- Receives stake deposits from staker via out of band platform
- Receives stake rewards from contracts on behalf of all stakers
- Distributes stake rewards via out of band platform
- Commission or fees charged may not be transparent

__`SSN operator` in phase 1__
- Operates the SSN
- Set commission fees on-chain
- Updates commission fees on-chain with restriction on the max commission rate change per reward cycle
- Receives commission on-chian
- No longer able to stake deposit

__`Delegator` in phase 1__
- Deposits stake directly into the staking smart contract
- Able to delegate to a SSN operator
- Can delegate to more than 1 SSN operator
- Able to redelegate to another SSN operator
- Withdraws rewards ($ZIL and gZIL) on-chain
- Withdraws stake deposit on-chain
